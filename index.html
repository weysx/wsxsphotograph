My philosophy

Welcome to explore the world through my lens!
Here, each photograph is a unique window into moments frozen in time. My motto, "The less available the more desirable", shapes how I approach photography. So I chase those fleeting, rare scenes. In a world flooded with images, I strive to present photos that feel exclusive, reminding viewers that true beauty often lies in what’s not easily seen or kept.

About Me

A guy who loves art. I love photography, embracing both digital and film. Digital lets me edit freely for vivid shots, while film charms me with its timeless grain and tonal richness. Whether using a modern or vintage camera, I aim to capture rare moments into lasting stories.
